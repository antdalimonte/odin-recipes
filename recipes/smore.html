S'mores

Who doesn't like S'mores?

Ingredients

  • Graham cracker
  • Chocolate bar
  • Marshmallow

Steps

  1. Heat a grill to medium-low heat, or use an open fire if available.
  2. Halve graham cracker and sandwich a portion of chocolate bar between two squares.
  3. Skewer marshmallow and hold over heat for 1-2 minutes or until golden brown.
  4. Using the top square of graham cracker, place marshmallow on chocolate and press the marshmallow down with cracker. Pull skewer out.
