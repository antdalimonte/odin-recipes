Instant Ramen Noodles

An inexpensive dish popular among the culinarily challenged

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Noodles
  • Flavor packet

Steps

  1. Bring 2½ cups of water to a boil in a small saucepan.
  2. Add the noodles and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add contents of flavor packet, stir, and continue to cook for another 30 seconds.
